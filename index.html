Young ambitious man, who came a long way to be where he is now. Originally, I am from a city in Poland called Elbląg, I came to the United Kingdom in 2014. Since I can remember I have always liked anything to do with computers and technology. Therefore, I decided that I would like to continue down this path and learn more about computer science.

In my free time, I like to play video games and spend time with my family and friends. I also love to watch movies and listen to music. My other passion other than computer science is DJing. Most of the days I spend a few hours a day, practising, finding new songs to mix and recording the session to find out what I have done right and what I must improve. As I am only 21 years old, I try to enjoy life and use every free minute to do things I like.

Throughout my three years at the University of Hertfordshire, by doing different coursework and essays and by spending my free time learning new programming languages I have gained more experience in programming languages like C++, Python, HTML/CSS, Java and JavaScript. My current objective is to obtain work experience in the Software Engineering field. I will be graduating in September 2022 with first-class honours, and I cannot wait to explore new possibilities.
